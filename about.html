About

Welcome to our D'tea & Coffee! Our shop was founded with a passion for bringing delicious and high-quality beverages to our customers.

We specialize in milk tea, offering a wide variety of flavors and toppings to suit every taste preference. Our milk tea is made with freshly brewed tea, combined with creamy milk and natural sweeteners. We take pride in using only the best quality ingredients.

Our frappes are the perfect treat for a hot day, made with rich espresso and blended with ice and milk. We offer a range of flavors, from classic mocha to fruity and refreshing options. Each frappe is topped with whipped cream and drizzled with your choice of syrup for a decadent finishing touch.

For those who prefer a classic cup of coffee, we offer freshly brewed coffee made with premium beans sourced from around the world. Our baristas are highly trained and skilled in creating the perfect cup of coffee, whether you prefer a simple drip or a more complex espresso-based drink.

We strive to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere in our shop, where our customers can relax and enjoy their drinks with friends and family. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff are always on hand to offer recommendations and answer any questions you may have.

Thank you for choosing our D'tea & Coffee as your go-to destination for delicious beverages. We look forward to serving you!
